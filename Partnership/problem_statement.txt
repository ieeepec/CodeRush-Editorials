Partnership
In an Australia vs India ODI match, Virender Sehwag and Harbhajan Singh guided Indian side 
to victory. While making the final scorecard, runs scored by each batsman got mixed up. 
It is known that Sehwag scored second highest runs while Harbhajan scored the fourth 
lowest score in the innings. 
It is also known that when Harbhajan came to bat, Sehwag had same runs as the runs scored 
by Harbhajan at the end of innings.
Calculate the runs they scored together in the match winning partnership.

Input:
The first line of input contains an integer T denoting the number of test cases. Next line contains 11 seperated integers denoting the runs scored by the Indian players (not necessarily in order).
Output: 
Print a single integer denoting the runs scored by Virender Sehwag and Harbhajan together.

Constrains:
1 <= T <= 100
0 <= runs <= 200

Example:
Sample Input:
2
70 20 49 110 9 98 52 33 43 0 51
80 30 59 120 90 78 62 23 33 10 41]


Sample Output:
70
90
